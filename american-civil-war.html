AMERICAN CIVIL WAR HISTORY

The American civil war was from 1861 to 1865. It started because the Northern(Union side) and the Southern( Confederacy side) United States had got many issues, such as slavery and westward expansion. By the time it ended in 1865, over 620 thousand of 2.4 million soldiers killed, millions people was injured and the South destroyed

1861

Outbreak of the civil war

The battle of Fort Sumter triggered the civil war on April 12

1862

The real civil war



The real fighting began in 1862. Huge battles such Shiloh in Tennessee, Gaines' Mill, Second Manassas, and Fredericksburg in Virginia, and Antietam in Maryland

1863

A emancipation proclamation



Lincoln based on the Union victory at Antietam to make the emancipation proclamation to free all slaves in rebellious states after January 1

1864

By 1864 the original Northern goal was restorative the Union. But it created a new strategy to destroy the Old South and its slavery and giving the restored Union a "new birth of freedom" as President Lincoln said at Gettysburg to memorize Union soldiers killed in the battle there

1865

End of war



By the spring of 1865 all the Confederate armies surrendered, and the war ended when Union cavalry captured the fleeing Confederate President Jefferson Davis in Georgia on May 10
